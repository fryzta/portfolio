Schibsted Next

Schibsted Next was an incubator within Schibsted where a team of ~30 people in Stockholm came up with new marketplace ideas and tried to make them to grow as fast as possible.

Some of the ideas were a second hand clothing app, an online car shop, and a second hand gift card marketplace.

We worked in several small lean teams consisting of a product owner, tech manager, designer/ux-designer and a user researcher.

Every project started of with a business model canvas, a lot of research including interviews, surveys, prototyping, experimenting and guerilla testing to validate every idea thoroughly.
