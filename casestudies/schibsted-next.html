Schibsted Next

Schibsted Next was an incubator within Schibsted, where a team of around 30 people in Stockholm focused on developing new marketplace ideas and accelerating their growth.

Some of the projects we worked on included a second-hand clothing app, an online car marketplace, and a platform for buying and selling second-hand gift cards.

We worked in small, lean teams made up of a product owner, tech manager, designer/UX designer, and user researcher.

Each project began with a business model canvas, followed by extensive research, including interviews, surveys, prototyping, experimentation, and guerrilla testing to thoroughly validate every idea.
