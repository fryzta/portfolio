Schibsted marketplaces

— Everyday stuff pattern.

I started working as a designer and front-end developer in Schibsted Classifieds Media Ventures in 2012.

We launched "clones" of the Swedish success Blocket in ~25 countries from Stockholm with a team of less than 30 people.

— Corotos, one of the largest websites in the dominican republic.

— Icons for many of the categories on the marketplaces.

The design and branding is a bit dated and rough due to the fact that we focused heavily on growth and quick experiments.

Many of the sites we launched are today some of the biggest sites in their respective countries and have had time to be improved on since.

I was the only designer in a team of 30 and did things like app design, ux, user testing, a/b-testing, marketing campaigns and prototyping.

— Mobile touch site focused on speed and simplicity.

We were forced to make some really simple sites for some countries, like Nigeria and Ghana who were behind when it comes to smart phones and internet speed. It was really fun and challenging to build sites with as high site speed as possible.
