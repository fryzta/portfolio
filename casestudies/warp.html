WARP

WARP is a multi-brand design system used by some of the largest marketplaces in the Nordics. I was part of a large cross-functional team, contributing as a visual designer to maintain and evolve the system for hundreds of designers and developers.

My work included everything from design tokens, components, and documentation to creating guidelines and contributing to leadership within the team.

Besides contributing as a visual designer I was also the design system lead for a time.

The internal documentation was the outcome of a month-long, cross-functional workshop that I led.

Its purpose is to guide both designers and developers in effectively using every aspect of the design system.
