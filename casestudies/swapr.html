Swapr

— Logotype symbol

Swapr was a game trading website that a couple of friends and I worked on in 2012.

Ultimately, the project was scrapped due to the rapid rise in digital game sales, which led us to believe there wasn't a long-term future for a product like this. However, it was still a fun and rewarding experience.
