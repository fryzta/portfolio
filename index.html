About

I’m a designer from the land of snow and blondes. I built my first website in the late ‘90s as a teenager and has worked with design ever since.

I thrive doing visual design but I have also done a fair share of user testing, user research and experimenting.

As of late my biggest passion has been design systems. For the last 3 years I’ve worked with a large multi-brand design system for some of the biggest brands in the nordics.

Even though I’m mainly designing stuff nowadays I do my best keeping my coding skills up to date by working on different hobby projects.
