About

I built my first website as a teenager in the late '90s, and I've been passionate about design ever since. While visual design is where I thrive, my experience also spans UX design, user testing, and user research.

In recent years, my biggest passion has been design systems. For the past three years, I've been working on a large, multi-brand design system for some of the biggest brands in the Nordics.

I’m a designer at heart, but I love keeping my coding skills sharp through various hobby projects. Bridging the gap between design and development is something I genuinely enjoy.
